Assistance at Home We give you the help you need to recover in the comfort of your own home. If you've been in hospital for more than three days, for any eligible condition on the plan, you'll receive help at home once you're discharged for things like personal care, help with getting washed and dressed and domestic duties such as general tidying and changing bed linen. Subject to a needs assessment, you can receive up to 28 hours' care per claim. If you make more than one claim in a plan year, a maximum of 84 hours' care in total applies.
